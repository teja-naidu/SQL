# Retail Sales and Customer Behavior Analysis

## Executive Summary

This analysis was performed on 99,457 retail transactions to understand customer purchasing behavior, revenue drivers, and business opportunities.

### Key Findings

- Total Revenue: $251.51 Million
- Total Transactions: 99,457
- Total Customers: 99,457
- Average Order Value: $2,528.79

---

## Revenue Analysis

### Top Revenue Generating Categories

| Category | Revenue |
|-----------|------------|
| Clothing | $113.99M |
| Shoes | $66.55M |
| Technology | $57.86M |

### Business Insight

Clothing alone contributes nearly 45% of total revenue, making it the primary revenue driver. Shoes and Technology are the next strongest categories.

---

## Shopping Mall Analysis

### Top Performing Shopping Malls

| Shopping Mall | Revenue |
|---------------|-----------|
| Mall of Istanbul | $50.87M |
| Kanyon | $50.55M |
| Metrocity | $37.30M |

### Business Insight

Mall of Istanbul and Kanyon generate significantly higher revenue than other malls, suggesting stronger customer traffic and spending behavior.

---

## Customer Analysis

### Revenue by Gender

| Gender | Revenue |
|---------|------------|
| Female | $150.21M |
| Male | $101.30M |

### Business Insight

Female customers contribute approximately 60% of total revenue and represent the most valuable customer segment.

---

## Age Group Analysis

| Age Group | Revenue |
|-----------|------------|
| 56+ | $67.29M |
| 36-45 | $50.13M |
| 46-55 | $48.19M |
| 26-35 | $47.83M |
| 18-25 | $38.08M |

### Business Insight

Customers aged 56+ generate the highest revenue. Revenue generally increases with age, indicating higher purchasing power among older customers.

---

## Payment Method Analysis

| Payment Method | Revenue |
|----------------|------------|
| Cash | $112.83M |
| Credit Card | $88.08M |
| Debit Card | $50.60M |

### Business Insight

Cash remains the dominant payment method, generating nearly 45% of total revenue.

---

## Customer Spending Behavior

### Average Spending by Gender

| Gender | Average Spending |
|---------|------------|
| Male | $2,534.05 |
| Female | $2,525.25 |

### Business Insight

Average order value is nearly identical between genders. Revenue differences are primarily driven by transaction volume rather than spending per purchase.

---

## Recommendations

1. Increase investment in Clothing inventory and promotions.
2. Focus marketing campaigns on female customers.
3. Develop loyalty programs targeting customers aged 45+.
4. Expand successful strategies used in Mall of Istanbul and Kanyon.
5. Continue supporting cash payment options while encouraging digital payments.
